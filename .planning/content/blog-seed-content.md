## Post 2: "From Waste to Biomaterials: The EKO City Story"

**Category:** Founder Stories
**Tags:** biotech, sustainability, circular-economy, biomaterials, startup-peru
**Author:** Alejandra Vera & Piero Beraun
**Language:** ES primary, EN translation

### Outline
1. The problem: Peru generates massive construction and agroindustrial waste. Styrofoam and single-use plastics persist in the environment
2. The insight: Mycelium (fungal root structures) can transform waste into biodegradable biomaterials — packaging, furniture, construction materials
3. The journey: From university lab (UTEC + UCSUR) to iGEM Design League champions to Falling Walls Peru winners
4. Technical approach: How mycelium-based biomaterials work (accessible explainer)
5. Milestones: 2 validated prototypes, 100 kg waste recovered, Startup Peru 11G, Exponential Inception 2024
6. The intersection with 404TF: How the pre-incubator ecosystem supports biotech startups in Peru
7. What's next: Scaling production, new material applications, fashion industry bioinks

### Key data points
- 2 biomaterial prototypes validated
- 100 kg waste recovered
- 1st place Falling Walls Peru 2023
- iGEM Design League LATAM champions (gold + 3 special prizes)
- Piero: Top 100 Leaders in Biotechnology in LATAM (Allbiotech)

## Post 3: "5 Things We Learned from 350+ Deeptech Entrepreneurs"

**Category:** Deep Dives
**Tags:** deeptech, mentorship, ecosystem, founders, latam
**Author:** 404 Tech Found Team
**Language:** ES primary, EN translation

### Outline
1. Context: Through the 404 Founders program and Deeptech Bootcamp, we've worked with 350+ entrepreneurs across AI, biotech, and hardware
2. Lesson 1: **The science is rarely the bottleneck** — the business model, regulatory path, and go-to-market strategy are where deeptech founders struggle most
3. Lesson 2: **Deeptech founders need a different kind of mentorship** — domain experts, not just startup generalists. A biotech founder needs someone who's navigated FDA-equivalent processes, not just lean canvas workshops
4. Lesson 3: **LATAM has world-class talent hiding in universities** — iGEM winners, published researchers, Beca 18 scholars. The pipeline exists, the infrastructure doesn't
5. Lesson 4: **Community beats competition** — the 404 Deeptech Meetup proved that founders in isolation need peers more than they need pitch practice
6. Lesson 5: **Start with the science, but ship like a startup** — "momentum over perfection" (404TF core value). The teams that progressed fastest combined scientific rigor with startup velocity
7. CTA: Apply to the next cohort / join the community

### Key data points
- 350+ entrepreneurs trained
- 500+ professionals connected at events
- 15 startups incubated
- 18 teams in cohort alpha
- Multiple grant winners in portfolio
