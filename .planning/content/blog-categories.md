# Blog Categories — 404 Tech Found

Custom categories + free-form tags (not tied to houses, per roadmap decision).

## Categories

### 1. Deep Dives
Technical deep dives into deeptech topics. Longer-form, educational content.
- Example: "How mycelium-based biomaterials replace styrofoam"
- Example: "Computer vision for marine monitoring: from ROV to production"
- Audience: Founders, researchers, technical readers

### 2. Ecosystem
LATAM deeptech ecosystem news, analysis, and commentary. Landscape mapping, funding trends, policy.
- Example: "The state of deeptech pre-incubation in Peru"
- Example: "How industrial policy shapes LATAM's EV supply chain"
- Audience: Investors, partners, government, founders

### 3. Founder Stories
Profiles and journeys of 404 founders and portfolio startups. The human side of deeptech.
- Example: "From university lab to Ginkgo Bioworks: the MikuyTec journey"
- Example: "Building an edtech startup on S/.0 — the Genera story"
- Audience: Aspiring founders, media, community

### 4. Events
Recaps, announcements, and takeaways from 404TF events and the broader LATAM deeptech scene.
- Example: "404 Deeptech Summit 2025: key takeaways"
- Example: "What we learned from 92 pre-incubator applications"
- Audience: Community, partners, media

### 5. Tutorials
Practical how-to content for deeptech founders. Fundraising, pitch decks, grant applications, technical tools.
- Example: "How to apply for Startup Peru: a step-by-step guide"
- Example: "Setting up your first biotech lab on a budget"
- Audience: Early-stage founders, students

### 6. News
Short-form updates. Announcements, milestones, partnerships, portfolio wins.
- Example: "EKO City selected for Exponential Inception 2024"
- Example: "404 Tech Found launches Deeptech Bootcamp"
- Audience: Everyone
