## Design Context

### Users
- **Primary**: Deep-tech founders & researchers (PhD students, postdocs) in LATAM — young, ambitious, scientifically rigorous but startup-naive. They need to feel "this is MY community."
- **Secondary**: VCs, corporate innovation leads, government agencies — need to see credibility, pipeline quality, and institutional trust.
- **Tertiary**: Media & general public — need excitement, a story worth covering.

### Brand Personality
**Maverick x Explorer** — rebellious, pioneering, boundary-pushing.
Three words: **Audacious. Rigorous. Alive.**
Emotional goals: belonging (founders), trust (investors), excitement (media).

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Reference**: BITKRAFT Ventures — cinematic restraint, scroll-based narrative, conviction in every pixel.
- **Anti-reference**: Generic startup pages with purple gradients, glassmorphism, "AI slop" aesthetics. Also: sterile academic institutional sites.
- **Theme**: Dark-first (most variants), but one light variant is valuable for formal/grant contexts.
- **Mascot (Tardi)**: Central to the brand — a tardigrade, the most resilient creature on Earth. Should feel alive and integrated, not tacked on.
- **Houses**: AI (#FF66C4 pink), Biotech (#00BF63 green), Hardware (#FFB400 amber) — each needs distinct identity within the unified brand.
- **Bilingual**: ES/EN — typography must work in both languages.

### Design Principles
1. **Earned attention**: Every element justifies its existence. No decoration without purpose.
2. **Science meets soul**: Rigor and warmth coexist. Not cold-institutional, not fluffy-startup.
3. **LATAM-rooted, globally legible**: Grounded in Latin American energy and warmth, but reads as world-class internationally.
4. **Tardi resilience**: The brand should feel indestructible, adaptive, alive — like its mascot.
5. **Signal over noise**: In a world of 404 errors, we ARE the signal. Clarity and conviction above all.
